In Alien Invasion, the player controls a ship that appears at the bottom of the screen.  The ship can move right and left using the arrow keys, and shoot with the space bar.

When the game begins, aliens fill the sky and move across the screen.  The player must shoot the aliens to destroy them. 

If the player is able to shoot all the aliens, a new fleet of aliens appear that move faster.  If any alien crashes with the player or is able to get to the bottom of the screen the player loses a ship.  If the player looses 3 thips the game ends.
